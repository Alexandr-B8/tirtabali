Why Canggu

Where rice fields meet the surf.

Once a string of quiet surf villages, Canggu has become Bali’s most easygoing hub — surf at dawn, work from a café by noon, and watch the sun drop into the sea with a drink in hand. Yet the rice fields and village temples are still right there between the cafes.

It’s a great base if you want the beach and the buzz without the polish of the deep south. We run sunset and west-coast tours from here, and a private driver makes the scattered cafes and breaks easy.

Local tips

Good to know before you go.

Best time to visit

Dry season (Apr–Oct) for sun; the surf is good much of the year, biggest in the dry months.

Getting around

Scooters rule here but traffic is real — a private driver is easiest for temples and far cafes.

Where to stay

Batu Bolong for the buzz, Berawa for families, Pererenan for a calmer edge.

Good to know

Carry cash for cafes, watch for rip currents, and swim at flagged beaches only.
