Travelling with kids

A few things that help.

Best for any age

Toddlers love the beaches and pools; from about 6, kids get more out of temples, waterfalls and the monkey forest.

What to pack

Reef-safe sunscreen, hats, swimmers, a light rain jacket and any meds. We bring the child seats and cold water.

Health & safety

Stick to bottled water, pack hand gel, and we'll build in shade and breaks. Pharmacies and clinics are easy to reach.

Food & fussy eaters

Warungs and cafés cater well to kids — rice, noodles, fruit, smoothies. Tell us allergies and we'll steer the day.

Parents ask

Family tours — your questions

Do you provide child seats?
Yes — baby and booster seats at no extra cost. Just tell us your children's ages when you book and we'll have the right ones fitted before we collect you.
Can we go at a slower pace with young kids?
That's the whole point of private. Late starts, naps in the car, long lunches — the day bends around your family, never a fixed timetable.
Are the tours safe for toddlers?
Yes. We pick gentle, shaded routes, build in regular breaks and skip anything strenuous. Tell us their ages and we'll tailor every stop to suit.
Is there a discount for children?
Little ones often join free or at a reduced rate, depending on the tour. Message us with their ages and we'll send you an exact family price.
What if the kids get tired or it rains?
We adapt on the spot — swap a temple for a pool, head back early, or reshuffle the day. Your guide always has a plan B in their back pocket.
