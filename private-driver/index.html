Good to ask

Private driver — your questions

Is the driver also a guide?
Yes — our drivers are local guides who'll share stories, take your photos and steer you to the good spots, not just drive. If you'd prefer a quiet ride, that's fine too.
Can we change the plan during the day?
Absolutely — it's your day. Add a stop, skip one, linger longer at lunch. Just tell your driver and they'll make it work.
How and when do we pay?
No prepayment. You pay cash or bank transfer at the end of the day, once you're happy. We confirm the price up front on WhatsApp.
Do you do late-night airport pickups?
Any hour. Send your flight details and we'll be waiting in arrivals with a name sign, and track the flight in case you're delayed.
What areas do you cover?
All of Bali, plus Nusa Penida via the fast boat. Pickups are island-wide — just tell us where you're staying.
